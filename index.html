Beth Keller Coaching

Everyone deserves a coach.

A brand-new teacher in 2006, my original training and first love was - and still is - Middle School. The best stuff is in the middle and you will never convince me otherwise. Leaving the classroom was a tough decision, but in 2018 the opportunity to increase of my impact as an instructional coach coaxed me out of my corner classroom.

As a coach, I quickly realized the majority of people already know what to do. They already have the answers. They already know the next right step. More often than not, they didn't need a new tool or a list of resources. Most educators (at ALL levels) just needed space - to think, consider, process, decide - a space to be heard, to reflect, to leave feeling encouraged and empowered.

I learned that a good coach creates and holds space for connection, curiosity, and growth.

I was hooked. I've been coaching - and being coached - ever since. I firmly believe in the power of coaching. I believe that every team, program, and leader can benefit from space to focus on their strengths, to ask thoughtful questions, and to encourage continual growth.

I have a coach. I think everyone deserves a coach. I would love for you to have one too.
